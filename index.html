SKILLS

What skills do I develop?

Creativity

Creativity is stimulated by combination of elements, visual innovation and experimentation

Concentration

Concentration is stimulated by creative process, use of specialized tools and time management

Perseverance

Perseverance is stimulated by problem resolution, clear objectives and planification

Key components
Techniques
Acrylic, oleo, watercolor painting and pastel chalks
Graphic Design
Learn the principles of graphic design, including color theory, typography, and composition. We have created projects, designs, and motion graphics.
Visual Arts
We explore painting, sculpture and digital art through practical projects, cultivating artistic skills and personal style.
Audiovisual Media
Gain expertise in video production, we will learn how to shoot, edit, and produce quality videos.
Storytelling
Develop the ability to tell compelling stories through visual media. This includes understanding narrative structure, visual storytelling techniques, and the use of technology to enhance production.
Project-Based Learning
Apply the skills learned through real-world projects. We will work on comprehensive projects that can be added to our portfolios.

Description

  • This specialization provides a comprehensive space to develop skills and knowledge in graphic design, visual arts and audiovisual media. Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical projects, students develop creativity, technical abilities and critical thinking skills.

Benefits

Career Opportunities:

Graduates will prepare us for careers in graphic design, visual arts, and audiovisual media, including roles such as graphic designer, art director, video editor, and multimedia artist.

Skill Development:

We’ll develop a strong foundation in design principles, artistic techniques, and audiovisual production, making them versatile and competitive in the job market.

Networking:

The specialization provides opportunities to connect with industry professionals, participate in workshops, and showcase work in exhibitions and online portfolios.
